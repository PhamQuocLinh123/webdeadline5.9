WHITE WATCH FACE SHOW MOON TIME

The white lacquered Cellini Moonphase dial with a blue enameled disc at 6 o'clock indicates a full moon and new moon, studded with meteorite and silver rings, respectively. The Moonphase version reads through indicators set at the 12 o'clock point, such as a full moon and a new moon revolving around the lunar cycle. The Cellini Moonphase version also displays the date around the perimeter of the dial, via the center hand with a crescent moon at its top.

Pearls always carry a mysterious image full of surprises from its own natural material. Depending on the origin of pearls, it can be pink, white, black or yellow. Differs in color, intensity and structure according to the parts of the shell from which it is extracted. At Rolex, pearls always retain their natural colours, without artificial staining.
